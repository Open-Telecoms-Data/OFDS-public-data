The Eletronet OFDS map is a hand-coded approximation of the network map at https://www.eletronet.com/rede/ (Internet Archive [copy](https://web.archive.org/web/20260118152455/https://www.eletronet.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/mapa-de-rede-eletronet-2024.webp)).  Some effort has been made to ensure that the Points of Presence are accurate but the network paths between the PoPs are purely notional.  
-Steve Song, 16 July 2025
